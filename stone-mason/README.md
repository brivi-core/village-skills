# 🪨 Stone Mason — Brivi Village Skill

> *Learning to cut, shape, and lay stone from the master masons of Tezi, Georgia.*
> *ქვის თლა-კვეთა — ტეზის ოსტატებისგან.*

## Status: Active — St. George Church, Tezi

Brivi Robot 1 begins its apprenticeship with the village stone masons on the construction of the new St. George Church, dedicated to Saint George (წმინდა გიორგი), standing next to the small church dedicated to Saint Nicholas (წმინდა ნიკოლოზი).

## Subskills

- [ ] Stone identification and selection
- [ ] Measuring and marking
- [ ] Cutting and shaping
- [ ] Mortar mixing (traditional Georgian recipe)
- [ ] Stone laying and alignment
- [ ] Cornerstone ceremony protocol
