# ⛪ St. George Church — Tezi, Georgia

> *The first real-world application of Brivi village skills.*
> *Brivi Robot 1 apprentices to the village stone masons building the new church dedicated to Saint George.*

## Context

The new St. George Church is under early-stage construction in the village of Tezi, Georgia. It stands next to the small existing church dedicated to Saint Nicholas (წმინდა ნიკოლოზი).

This is where Brivi Robot 1 learns its first village skill — stone masonry — from the Tezi masters. The church construction provides:

- **Real teaching** — Village masons demonstrate traditional techniques
- **Real work** — Robot assists with lifting, holding, and eventually laying stone
- **Real purpose** — Building a sacred space for the community
